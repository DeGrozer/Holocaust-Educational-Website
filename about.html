ABOUT

This topic is one that I deemed important to remember because it highlights one of the darkest periods in human history. The Holocaust stands as a reminder of just how much suffering humanity is capable of inflicting when prejudice, discrimination and indifference run rampant. By exploring this historical event through writing on my blog I hope to pay tribute to millions who died tragically while also raising awareness about the magnitude of this event. I hope to foster tolerance and inspire motivation towards creating a world where such atrocities will be prevented once and for all.

ABOUT ME


My name is Jan Andrew R. Barte, and I am a freshman student at Camarines Sur Polytechnic Colleges. I am currently taking up Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. One of my hobbies is researching niche topics that interests me, especially history. I find it enjoyable to study the past and learn something from it. I'm also fond of watching movies and documentaries, and listening to music.

Growing up, I dreamed of studying Political Science and eventually becoming a diplomat. I was passionate about public service and wanted to represent my country on the global stage. However, due to the COVID-19 pandemic, I never got the chance to take the entrance exam for a university that offered the program. It was a dream deferred, but not forgotten.

Instead of letting that setback define me, I chose to channel my love for history and global affairs into this project. The Holocaust represents one of the most significant events in human history, and by creating this website, I found a way to incorporate my interests while paying tribute to the millions who suffered.

In my free time, I like to take pictures, read and write poems and stories. I find it a good way of expanding and expressing my creativity and write about different topics and ideas. Plus, I enjoy watching sports such as volleyball and table tennis.
